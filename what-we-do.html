🌾 Agro-nomy Business

Structuring the Future of Livestock-Integrated Agriculture

At Vaishnavi Dairy and Milk Products, we are building a new economic layer in rural India — one where agriculture, livestock, energy, and community livelihoods operate in synergy.

We call this model Agro-nomy — a term we use to define structured, multi-output, high-efficiency agri-enterprise that is both investor-compliant and field-executable. Unlike conventional farming, Agro-nomy is not seasonal, not single-source, and not vulnerable to market shocks. It is a stable, vertically integrated business with optimized use of resources, managed operations, and planned revenue diversity.

🧠 The Business Logic Behind Agro-nomy

Agro-nomy was developed to address five major inefficiencies in rural agriculture:

  • 1. Fragmented operations with no revenue continuity
  • 2. Dependency on subsidies or middlemen for returns
  • 3. Land underutilization with poor yield per acre
  • 4. Wastage of animal by-products like dung, urine, and feed residue
  • 5. Lack of compliance-ready structures for investment or scale

Our model solves this through end-to-end control, asset productivity, and multi-use infrastructure — all legally, financially, and technically structured for scale.

💡 How Agro-nomy Generates Value

  • Livestock Unit = Predictable cashflow via structured fattening, breeding, and off-take cycles
  • Feed Unit = Cost arbitrage by in-house production with surplus market sales
  • Fodder System = High-yield, water-efficient, year-round production reducing grazing needs
  • Biogas Unit = Energy cost recovery + organic manure sales + carbon-saving opportunities
  • Farmer Network = Low-cost input supply via buyback tie-ups and contract rearing

Each of these units is interdependent yet independently profitable, which ensures business continuity regardless of external volatility.

📈 Strategic Advantages for Investors

  • 🏗️ Bankable Assets – Fixed infrastructure, livestock capital, and land-based productivity
  • 📊 Real-Time Traceability – For livestock cycles, feed conversion, and herd health
  • 📅 Cashflow Calendar – Structured sales and income mapping across 12 months
  • ✔️ Compliant & Auditable – Built to align with FPO frameworks, NABARD norms, and CSR mandates
  • 📦 Scalable in Pods – Replicable 5–10-acre units, adaptable to different geographies

This is not just a subsidy-led project — this is a returns-led, system-based business with organic scalability.

🏛️ Agro-nomy as a National Model

  • 🌐 Agri-Clusters & Rural Industrial Parks
  • 👨‍🌾 FPO-driven Cooperative Agribusiness
  • ⚡ Decentralized Bio-Energy Networks

  • 💼 Social Impact Ventures with Investor Exit Potential

Our focus is not just to succeed — but to set a replicable benchmark that can transform rural India into a network of integrated, revenue-positive agri-enterprises.

♻️ Biogas Generation

Turning Waste into Wealth — Thermal & Electrical Energy for a Circular Livestock Economy

At Vaishnavi Dairy and Milk Products, sustainability isn’t a slogan — it’s a system. Our integrated Biogas Generation Unit is a central pillar of our closed-loop livestock model, transforming organic waste into clean, usable energy — both thermal (heat) and electrical — for our farm operations and beyond.

🔋 Dual-Purpose Energy Output

  • ⚡ Electricity: Powers Fodder units, lighting, pellet plant, and farm equipment
  • 🔥 Thermal Energy: Boils milk, sterilizes equipment, and supports sanitation
  • 📊 Capacity: 25 kW+ output from a 500 m³ biogas digester, scalable on demand
  • 🧪 Input: Livestock dung, Agro-waste and Fodder residue from daily operations

🌿 Full Circular Economy Integration

  1. Livestock waste is collected and funnelled into the biogas digester
  2. Biogas is generated and routed to thermal applications and electricity conversion
  3. Slurry/manure is processed into organic fertilizer and compost
  4. Fertilizer enriches Hydroponic fodder units and allied cropping
  5. Feed & fodder go back to livestock — completing the sustainability cycle

💼 Why Investors Should Care

  • 💸 Energy Cost Recovery – Reduces diesel/electricity costs by up to 40%
  • 📈 ESG-Aligned & Compliant – Aligned with global sustainability goals, carbon credit initiatives, and NABARD grants
  • 🌍 Zero Waste, Zero Emissions – Captures methane, cuts GHG emissions
  • 🔁 Multiplies Margin – Converts waste into recurring value powering key farm units
  • 📋 Auditable, Scalable Asset – Grid-isolated, modular for replication, ideal for CSR/ESG investment

🔧 Strategically Engineered

  • 🔄 Built to process waste from 900+ livestock daily
  • 📡 Operates under SOPs with digital metering and safety controls
  • 🧬 Integrated with Feed, Fodder and manure cycles
  • 📊 Adds long-term financial predictability through fuel savings and secondary sales

From lighting our barns to boiling 10,000+ liters of milk/month, our biogas plant is the invisible engine powering a high-performance and investor-friendly livestock system.

Biogas: Powering Our Future

Our Biogas Plant is designed to convert various types of organic waste — including goat and sheep dung, agricultural residues, and organic matter — into valuable biogas through the process of anaerobic digestion. The generated biogas is then utilized to produce electricity, cooking gas, and organic manure, making our entire operation energy-efficient and environmentally responsible.

Our Biogas System Includes:

  • Turnkey Bio-Waste to Electricity Solutions:
    We have adopted a complete "Bio-Waste to Energy" model at our farm. By processing organic wastes from our goat and sheep rearing activities, we generate biogas that powers our operations.
    Electricity Generation Capacity: Our decentralized biogas plant can generate electricity ranging from 3 kW to 1000 kW per hour, supporting various farm operations like water pumping, lighting, fodder processing, and milk chilling.
    Byproducts: Alongside energy, we also produce organic manure, enhancing soil fertility for our fodder cultivation, and cooking gas for on-site use.
  • Construction of Advanced Biogas Digesters:
    Our Biogas Plant includes a professionally constructed fixed dome-type digester, built with cutting-edge technology by a highly skilled team.
    Capacity: Single unit capacity ranging from 20 m³ to 1000 m³.
    Integrated Design: Our setup includes an Input Feed Tank (for mixing dung and agricultural waste) and an Output Tank (for collecting nutrient-rich organic slurry).
    This infrastructure enables continuous production of biogas, ensuring energy self-reliance and efficient waste management.
  • Biogas Generator Systems:
    We use a specially designed Biogas Generator, capable of delivering reliable electricity without reliance on fossil fuels.
    Capacity Range: 3 kVA to 125 kVA.
    Fuel: 100% powered by biogas and biomass fuels.
    Durability: Built to withstand extreme weather conditions with minimal maintenance, ensuring dependable, clean power supply for farm operations.
  • Scrubber and Filtration Technology:
    Our plant is equipped with advanced Scrubbers and Biogas Filtration Systems that remove unwanted gases like H₂S, CO₂, and moisture from the raw biogas.
    Metal Scrubbers offer partial purification.
    Complete Biogas Filtration ensures cleaner, more efficient gas for generators and cooking purposes.

Advantages of Integrating Biogas Technology in Our Goat & Sheep Farming

  • Economic Advantages:
    Zero-Cost Fuel: A one-time investment ensures a continuous, renewable energy supply without ongoing fuel costs.
    Organic Manure Revenue: High-quality organic fertilizer derived from the digester output is used in our fodder fields or sold commercially.
    Savings on Waste Disposal: Efficient waste recycling reduces waste management costs.
    Off-Grid Power Independence: We are self-reliant for our electricity needs, avoiding rising grid energy costs.
  • Environmental Advantages:
    Reduction in Greenhouse Gases: Our plant helps minimize methane emissions from unmanaged waste.
    Lower Carbon Footprint: Every unit of biogas used reduces dependence on non-renewable resources.
    Eco-Friendly Waste Management: We transform waste into valuable resources instead of letting it pollute land and water.
    Organic Fertilizer Production: Enhances soil health and reduces chemical fertilizer use, promoting sustainable agriculture.
  • Social Advantages:
    Health and Cleanliness: Proper waste management leads to a cleaner and safer environment around the farm.
    Rural Empowerment: Creates employment opportunities in construction, operation, and maintenance of biogas plants.
    Self-Employment Opportunities: Encourages sustainable farming practices among nearby rural communities.
    Community Leadership: We set a model for environmentally conscious livestock farming in Uttarakhand and beyond.

Biogas Plant for Renewable Energy at M/S Vanam Enterprises

Project Overview

At M/S Vanam Enterprises, we are proud to introduce a state-of-the-art 500 m³ Biogas Plant designed to sustainably harness energy from animal waste at our integrated farm in Dehradun, Uttarakhand.
With a thriving livestock base of 100 HF cows, 100 buffalos, 500 goats, and 500 sheep, our farm generates an average of 5–6 tons of organic feed material daily, providing an abundant resource for renewable energy production.
Our Biogas Plant converts this organic waste into:
- Electricity (1 m³ Biogas = 1.5 kWh)
- Thermal Energy (1 m³ Biogas = 450 g LPG equivalent)
- Organic Manure (natural fertilizer for agriculture)
This initiative positions us at the forefront of sustainable farming, waste management, and green energy generation in Uttarakhand.

"Our biogas system turns waste into wealth — powering farms while healing the planet."

  • ♻ Production of Organic Fertilizer — enhances soil fertility and supports sustainable agriculture.
  • ⚡ Distributed Power Generation (DPG) — on-site electricity production, minimizing transmission losses and boosting energy security.
